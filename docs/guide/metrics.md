# Metrics & Analytics

Track your email performance with real-time metrics and analytics.

## Dashboard Overview

The home dashboard shows key metrics for the last 30 days:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Emails Sent** | Total emails sent across all domains |
| **Active Domains** | Number of verified domains |
| **Credit Balance** | Remaining email credits |
| **Bounce Rate** | Percentage of emails that bounced |
| **Complaint Rate** | Percentage of spam complaints |
| **Suppressions** | Total suppressed addresses |

## Health Status

Your account health is determined by bounce rate:

| Status | Bounce Rate | Recommendation |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Healthy** | < 3% | You're doing great |
| **Warning** | 3-4% | Review your email list quality |
| **Danger** | > 4% | Take immediate action to reduce bounces |

::: warning High Bounce Rate
A bounce rate over 5% can harm your sender reputation and may result in sending restrictions. Clean your email lists regularly.
:::

## Improving Metrics

### Reduce Bounces

- Verify email addresses before adding to your list
- Use double opt-in for subscriptions
- Remove inactive subscribers
- Check for typos in email addresses

### Reduce Complaints

- Only send to people who opted in
- Include clear unsubscribe links
- Set proper expectations about email frequency
- Make your sender name recognizable

### Maintain Good Health

- Keep bounce rate under 3%
- Keep complaint rate under 0.1%
- Monitor metrics regularly
- Act quickly on warning signs
